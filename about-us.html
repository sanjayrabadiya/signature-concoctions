About Me

Hello!

Thank you for being here!

My name is Wafa and I’m the food blogger behind Archived Chef. I’ve been cooking alongside my mother since I was in my early teens, overtime I started taking more control in the kitchen.

In 2020, I was introduced to tik tok by my siblings who would consistently tell me to download it because it was ‘funny.’ I finally caved and downloaded it and as I was watching I came across food videos on my for you page and I thought to myself, “I can do this too.”

A couple of months after having my daughter,  in the beginning of March 2020, I did it. I posted my first food video. A few videos later, I got my first viral video at 2.3 million views, which only pushed me to work harder! 3 years later, I’ve reached 240k followers on tiktok and 100k on instagram and I couldn’t be happier. Cooking and baking has always been my passion and it’s my way of showing love to family and friends. Every compliment and rave I’ve received from the ones around me fill my heart with absolute joy. I’m so glad I can also share it online with the world and whoever my recipes stumble upon.
